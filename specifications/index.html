The Feeling

Experience

From the moment you step into the grand lobby, the estate commands attention. Soaring ceilings, a private lift that connects every floor, and an uninterrupted visual dialogue between interior craftsmanship and the Singapore skyline beyond.

Designed for those who entertain with intention, the full-floor entertainment level (L5) offers a rooftop terrace with panoramic Marina Bay Sands and city skyline views—a stage for sunsets, dinners, and unforgettable gatherings.

At ground level, the lobby lounge—anchored by a grand piano—sets a tone of refined elegance. Up through the private lift, each floor reveals a new chapter: from a chef's kitchen and long-table dining terrace to the serene owner's suite framing the city skyline.

The estate is move-in ready, with EV charging, parking for up to 5 vehicles internally plus roadside access, and every detail resolved.

Floor by Floor

The Levels

L1

Grand Lobby & Piano Lounge

An entrance unlike any other. The ground floor lobby commands presence with soaring double-height ceilings, marble finishes, and a resident grand piano. The private lift—accessed from here—connects seamlessly to every upper level.

Sofa area was virtually staged. Lift, grand piano, ceiling and lights are original.

L2

Chef's Kitchen, Long-Table Dining & Terrace Pond

The social heart of the estate. A fully equipped chef's kitchen flows into a generous long-table dining room—ideal for formal dinners and private gatherings. Step onto the terrace where a serene reflecting pond and lush greenery blur the line between inside and garden.

L3

Owner's Suite with MBS & Skyline Views

The owner's suite occupies the full third floor, featuring a private walk-in wardrobe, a spa-inspired ensuite bathroom, and floor-to-ceiling windows with sweeping Marina Bay Sands and city skyline views. A sanctuary of calm above the city.
